চিত্র: একটি অসম্পূর্ণ কবিতা
a quiet afternoon in Jorasanko...

They say the poet wrote about every shade of human emotion. He mapped the monsoons of Bengal and the depths of solitary longing. But standing here today, surrounded by his legacy, I realized something profound.

He never got to write about you.

গীতবিতান

He never saw the way you absently fix the pleats of your saree when you are deep in thought. He never heard the quiet, almost imperceptible 'hm' you make when reading a line you love.

If he had seen your smile catching the golden hour light in a North Kolkata alleyway, the Gitabitan would have been twice as thick. Entire stanzas would have been dedicated just to the way you look away when caught staring.

and so, I must write it myself.
অলিখিত কবিতা
তোমার শহরে আজ শ্রাবণের মেঘ,
আমার খাতায় শুধু শূন্যতার দাগ।
রবিঠাকুরের গানে তোমায় খুঁজি,
তুমি তো আমার না-বলা অনুরাগ।
In your city, the monsoon clouds gather,
On my notebook, only stains of emptiness.
I search for you in Tagore's melodies,
You are my unspoken devotion.

অভিমান

The way your silence speaks louder than any storm. Your sweet, stubborn anger that I would spend lifetimes trying to soothe.

কাজল

The smudged kohl under your eyes after a long day. It doesn't ruin the poetry; it simply underlines the most important parts.

ছন্দ

You don't walk into a room; you occur to it. Like a sudden change in rhythm in the middle of a familiar song.

স্পর্শ

The accidental graze of your hand against mine. A fleeting collision of universes that rewrote my entire history.

মায়া

The careless way you gather your hair into a messy bun. An effortless illusion that somehow holds all my gravity.

তুমি

You are not just a person.

You are an unfinished manuscript,

breathing quietly in the archives of my soul.

A verse I will spend forever trying to memorize.

শ্রাবণ

শ্রাবণের চিঠি

I wrote a hundred letters in my mind. Each one dissolved by the torrential rain of Sravana before it could reach the paper. They say unexpressed love eventually turns into art. But mine didn't turn into art. It turned into a quiet longing, waiting by the rusted window grilles of an old North Kolkata house, listening to the rain.

some letters are only meant to be felt.
ইতি...

So let the city celebrate the poet today. Let them sing his songs.

In the quiet sanctuary of my thoughts, I am only celebrating the poetry that exists because you do.

forever, yours.
